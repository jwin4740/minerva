CHESS and/or COMPUTER SCIENCE NEW TERMINOLOGY
---------------------------------------------

ply - a half move in chess

perft divider - number of leaf nodes per move
            - you can find perft dividers online
            example: sharper
horizon effect - for chess engines; if the engine finishes the search on its move, it tends
                to bring the queen out and capture a piece to get the best score; disregarding
                that it may lose its queen next turn
